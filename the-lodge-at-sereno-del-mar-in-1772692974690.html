Welcome to Your Bodega Bay Stay

Escape to the tranquil coastal town of Bodega Bay, where the soothing sound of ocean waves meets the serene beauty of the surrounding ranch. Our home is nestled in a quiet neighborhood, just a short stroll from Portuguese Beach and surrounded by breathtaking views of the ocean. Take a moment to unwind and recharge in this peaceful retreat, and then venture out to explore the numerous beaches, hiking trails, and attractions that await you.
