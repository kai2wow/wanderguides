Welcome to Waianae on the Island of Oahu

Aloha and welcome to our home in Waianae! We're located on the western coast of Oahu, just a short drive from the ocean. Our home is a cozy two-story house with a black rod iron gate. You can park on the grass or inside the driveway when there's space to do so. We're excited to have you stay with us and explore the beautiful island of Oahu.

At Wander Well, we believe that travel is not just about seeing new places, but also about giving back to the communities we visit. Our mission is to re-imagine travel through service, connecting guests like you with local organizations and people who are making a difference.

How to Use This Volunteer Guide
