Welcome to The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar

The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar is a serene retreat located just 4 houses down from Portuguese Beach in Bodega Bay, CA. This tranquil oasis sits on half an acre and backs up to a 600-acre ranch, offering stunning views of the ocean and surrounding landscape. As you relax on the back patio, you can often spot cows visiting in the mornings, and the sound of the ocean waves provides a soothing background melody.
