Welcome to Waianae on the Island of Oahu

Aloha and welcome to our home in Waianae! We're excited to have you stay with us and experience the beauty of the island. Our grey and white two-story house with a black rod iron gate is conveniently located near local restaurants and amenities. Just drive past the restaurant and take the first driveway on your right-hand side to find us. You can park on the grass or inside the driveway when there's space available.
