Welcome to Waianae on the Island of Oahu

Aloha and welcome to Waianae on the Island of Oahu! Our cozy two-story home is nestled in a charming coastal area, perfect for relaxation and exploration. As you settle in, take a moment to appreciate the beautiful ocean views and the warmth of our community. Just drive past the restaurant and take the first driveway on your right-hand side – you'll find us with our black rod iron gate.
