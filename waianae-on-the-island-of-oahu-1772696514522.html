Welcome to Waianae on the Island of Oahu

Aloha and warm welcome to our home in Waianae! We're located just off the beaten path, past the restaurant and the first driveway on your right-hand side. You'll know us by our Grey/White two-story house with a black rod iron gate. Please park on the grass or inside the driveway when space allows. We can't wait to share our little slice of island life with you.

Purpose-Driven Travel Begins Here

At WanderWell, we re-imagine travel through service. We believe that by giving back to the communities we visit, we can create a more meaningful, inclusive, and sustainable travel experience for everyone.
