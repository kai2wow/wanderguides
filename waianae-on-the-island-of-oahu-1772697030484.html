Welcome to Waianae on the Island of Oahu

Aloha! We're so glad you're staying with us in Waianae. Our cozy home is located in a beautiful coastal area, and we're just a short drive from some amazing volunteer opportunities. We hope you enjoy your stay and have the chance to give back to this wonderful community.
