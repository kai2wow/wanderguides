Welcome to The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar

Escape to the serene coastal town of Bodega Bay, where our home, The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar, awaits. Located just a short stroll from Portuguese Beach, our property offers breathtaking views of the ocean and surrounding 600-acre ranch. Spend your mornings sipping coffee on the deck, watching the cows roam freely in the nearby fields. With its tranquil atmosphere and easy access to hiking trails, beaches, and tourist attractions, our home is the perfect haven for relaxation and exploration.
