Welcome to Waianae on the Island of Oahu

Aloha! Welcome to our home in beautiful Waianae, where the ocean meets the countryside. We're so glad you're here to experience the warmth and hospitality of our community. As you explore the area, you'll find a rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and a strong sense of aloha spirit. Our home is conveniently located near the beach, with plenty of opportunities to enjoy the sun, sand, and surf.

Purpose-Driven Travel Begins Here

At WanderWell, we believe that travel should be a journey of discovery and connection. That's why we've curated this volunteer guide to help you make a positive impact in our community and learn about the amazing work of local organizations.

🤝

Connect

Meet the people and organizations making a difference in Waianae and learn about their stories and missions.

🌱

Ground

Immerse yourself in the local culture and landscape, and explore the natural beauty of the Waianae Coast.

💛

Impact

Make a tangible difference in the lives of our community members and contribute to the well-being of our island home.

✨

Inspire

Discover new passions and interests, and be inspired by the creativity, resilience, and aloha spirit of our community.

At WanderWell, we re imagine travel through service, empowering guests to make a positive impact in the communities they visit. By volunteering and engaging with local organizations, our guests not only contribute to the well-being of the community but also gain a deeper understanding of the local culture and way of life.

How to Use This Volunteer Guide

Follow these simple steps to find and participate in meaningful volunteer opportunities during your stay.

1

Browse Categories

Explore the different categories and find organizations that align with your interests and passions.

2

Make Impact
