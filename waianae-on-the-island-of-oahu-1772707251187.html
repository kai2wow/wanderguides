Welcome to Waianae on the Island of Oahu

Aloha and welcome to our home on the beautiful Waianae Coast! We're the grey and white two-story house with the black rod iron gate—just drive past the restaurant and take the first driveway on your right. You can park on the grass or in the driveway when space is available. This side of Oahu is known for its authentic island spirit, stunning sunsets over Pokai Bay, and a tight-knit community that welcomes visitors with open hearts.

Purpose-Driven Travel Begins Here

The Waianae Coast thrives on the spirit of ohana and aloha ʻāina—love for family and land. This guide connects you with local organizations where your time and energy can make a meaningful difference in this special community.

🤝

Connect

Meet the heart of the Waianae Coast community through grassroots organizations serving families, veterans, and keiki (children) with true aloha spirit.

🌱

Ground

Reconnect with the ʻāina (land) through sustainable agriculture, beach restoration, and honoring Native Hawaiian traditions of environmental stewardship.

💛

Impact

Support vital services addressing food security, housing, and youth development in one of Oahu's most culturally rich and resilient communities.

✨

Inspire

Experience the profound mana (spiritual power) of giving back in a place where ancient Hawaiian values of lōkahi (unity) and kuleana (responsibility) still guide daily life.

At WanderWell, we believe travel becomes transformative when we give back to the places that host us. Every volunteer hour you contribute helps strengthen the Waianae community and deepens your connection to the true spirit of Hawaii.

How to Use This Volunteer Guide

Follow these simple steps to find and participate in meaningful volunteer opportunities during your stay.

1

Browse Categories

Explore opportunities across food security, community support, environmental care, and more. Each organization has been carefully selected for its local impact and welcoming approach to volunteers.

2

Check Schedules

Visit organization websites or call ahead to confirm volunteer hours, current needs, and any special requirements. Many programs welcome drop-ins, while others prefer advance coordination.

3

Sign Up

Reach out directly to the organizations that resonate with you. Let them know you're a WanderWell guest and share how much time you'd like to contribute.

4

Make Impact

Show up with an open heart and willing hands. Even a few hours of service can create lasting positive change and give you memories far beyond typical tourism.

Pro Tip: Scroll down to see our interactive map with all volunteer locations pinned—perfect for planning opportunities near your daily adventures!
