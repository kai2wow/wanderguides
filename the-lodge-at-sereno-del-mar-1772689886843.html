Welcome to The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar

Escape to our serene coastal haven in Bodega Bay, where the sound of ocean waves and the gentle visits of our friendly cows create the perfect retreat. With easy access to stunning beaches, hiking trails, and picturesque towns like San Francisco, Russian River, and Napa, our home is the ideal base for your next adventure.
