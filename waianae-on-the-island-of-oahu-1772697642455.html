Welcome to Waianae on the Island of Oahu

Aloha and warm welcome to our home in Waianae on the beautiful island of Oahu. As you settle in, we invite you to explore the incredible community that surrounds us. From the stunning coastline to the lush interior, Waianae is a haven for those who love the outdoors and want to make a difference.

Purpose-Driven Travel Begins Here

In this volunteer guide, we're excited to share with you the many opportunities to give back while staying with us in Waianae. Whether you're interested in food, animals, community, environment, or creativity, we've curated a selection of organizations that are making a real difference in our community.

🤝

Connect

Meet new people and build relationships with locals and like-minded travelers.

🌱

Ground

Immerse yourself in the local culture and learn about the history and customs of the area.

💛

Impact

Contribute to meaningful projects and make a positive impact on the community.

✨

Inspire

Be inspired by the passion and dedication of the local community and the incredible work being done here.

At Wander Well, we believe that travel should be about more than just seeing new places - it should be about making a difference in the world. That's why we're committed to reimagining travel through service, and we're excited to share this vision with you.

How to Use This Volunteer Guide

Follow these simple steps to find and participate in meaningful volunteer opportunities during your stay.

1

Browse Categories

Explore the different areas of interest and find the organizations that resonate with you.

2

Check Schedules

Look at the volunteer schedules and plan your activities in advance.

3

Sign Up

Choose the organizations and activities that you'd like to participate in and sign up.

4

Make Impact

Get out there and make a difference - we can't wait to hear about your experiences!

Pro Tip: Be sure to scroll down to see our interactive volunteer map with all locations pinned - it's a great way to visualize the opportunities available to you.
