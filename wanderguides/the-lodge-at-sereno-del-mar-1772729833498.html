Welcome to The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar

Your coastal sanctuary awaits just four houses down from Portuguese Beach, where the morning fog rolls in over 600 acres of ranchland and curious cows often greet you on the back patio. This serene half-acre retreat offers the perfect blend of oceanside tranquility and adventure—listen to the waves from your deck, stroll down the private beach staircase across the road, or venture out to nearby hiking trails, whale watching spots, and the treasures of Sonoma County. Whether you're here to unwind with coffee overlooking the Pacific or explore the stunning coastline, you've found your home away from home.

How to Use This Volunteer Guide

Follow these simple steps to find and participate in meaningful volunteer opportunities during your stay.

1

Browse Categories

Explore opportunities across food security, wildlife care, environmental stewardship, community building, and creative programs. Each organization has been carefully vetted for meaningful volunteer experiences.

2

Check Schedules

Visit organization websites or call ahead to confirm volunteer hours and current needs. Many coastal nonprofits welcome drop-in volunteers, while others require advance registration.

3

Sign Up

Reach out directly to your chosen organization to reserve your volunteer slot. Let them know you're a WanderWell guest staying nearby and excited to help.

4

Make Impact

Show up with an open heart and helpful hands. Even a few hours can make a real difference to these vital community organizations.

Pro Tip: Scroll down to explore our interactive map—every volunteer location is pinned so you can easily plan your giving-back adventure around Bodega Bay and Sonoma County.
