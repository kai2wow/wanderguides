Welcome to The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar

Welcome to The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar, a coastal retreat nestled in the heart of Bodega Bay. Just steps from Portuguese Beach, our home sits on half an acre and offers breathtaking views of a sprawling 600-acre ranch with the shimmering Pacific Ocean beyond. Whether you’re relaxing on the back patio or exploring the nearby shoreline, your stay promises serenity and stunning natural beauty at every turn.

Purpose-Driven Travel Begins Here

As a guest at The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar, we invite you to give back to the local community by volunteering with one of the many organizations in the area. This guide will help you discover opportunities to make a positive impact during your stay.

Connect

Meet like-minded individuals and build relationships with the local community.

Ground

Get involved in environmental conservation efforts and help protect the natural beauty of the area.

Impact

Make a meaningful difference in the lives of others by volunteering with organizations that support the local community.

Inspire

Be inspired by the dedication and passion of local organizations working to create positive change.

At WanderWell, we believe that travel and giving back go hand-in-hand. Our mission is to provide opportunities for guests to engage with local communities and make a positive impact on the places they visit.

Who they are

Sonoma Land Trust is a nonprofit organization dedicated to protecting and restoring native habitats, creeks, wildlife corridors, and productive farms and ranches in Sonoma County.
