Welcome to The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar

Our home is 4 houses down from Portuguese Beach. It sits on half an acre and backs up to a 600 acre ranch. The back patio looks out over the ranch with the ocean behind it. I love that the cows often come and visit us in the mornings while we are drinking our coffee on the deck. It is a very quiet and serene place. You can hear the sound of the ocean waves from the house. There is a staircase that goes down to the beach right across the road from the neighborhood. There are many other beaches and hiking trails close by. The house is not far from other tourist locations such as San Francisco, Russian River, and Napa. During March and April you can sometimes see the whale migrating in the distance. It’s a fantastic house to stay and relax or venture out. The home has 2 bedrooms with cal king beds, 1 queen pullout sofa bed and 1 queen Murphy bed in the game room(which we have listed as a 3rd bedroom). There is a queen blowup mattress as well and a portable crib. There are 2 bathrooms.
